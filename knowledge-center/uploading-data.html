Submitting Data to TDP

Once your request for access has been approved by an OFA admin your account will have access to the ‘Data Files’ page where you’ll be able to submit your quarterly transmission files.

How to submit data

  1. Choose which reporting period you’ll be submitting for


    On the Data Files page you’ll be able to fill out the search fields for ‘Fiscal Year’ and ‘Quarter’ to identify which data you’ll be uploading. After you’ve searched for the appropriate period a new section will appear on your page.

  2. Upload your files


    You can either drag each file into their appropriate sections (Active, Closed, Aggregate, and Stratum) or click ‘Choose from folder’ on a section to browse your computer for the file.

    Note: Section 4 is only required for grantees who have elected to submit a stratified sample of families served in their jurisdiction rather than data for all families.


  3. Submit your files


    Once you click the ‘Submit Data Files’ button you should see a confirmation that your sections were submitted and OFA will have access to your files!

    In the future TDP will provide more immediate reports about your data, but for the moment you should expect to receive the ones you’re used to receiving via email within 48 hours from submission.

Retrieving your data

Once you click the ‘Submit Data Files’ button you should see a confirmation that your sections were submitted and OFA will have access to your files!

If you need to access a file you’ve uploaded to TDP you can click the ‘Download Section’ button below any submitted file. For now this will only provide you the most recent version of that file (if you’ve submitted more than one) TDP does store each submitted file and should you need to access one please feel free to reach out to tanfdata@acf.hhs.gov.

Note: Future versions of TDP will include a ‘Submission History’ page where you’ll be able to access all your past submitted files directly.

Resubmitting your data

If you need to replace or revise any file submitted to TDP you can do so either by dragging a new file into the section(s) you’d like to resubmit or clicking ‘Change File’. Once the new file(s) are selected make sure to click the ‘Submit Data Files’ button again to save your changes!
